4. European Economic Area (GDPR) — Consent (UMP)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK and Switzerland: We use Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request your consent before showing personalized advertising. You can choose:
  • Personalized ads
  • Non-personalized ads
  • Manage vendors
You may withdraw or modify your consent at any time from the in-app privacy settings. Our consent mechanism follows the IAB Europe Transparency & Consent Framework (TCF). Legal basis for processing:
  • Consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a))
  • Legitimate interest (limited measurement & fraud prevention)

5. United States Privacy Rights (CPRA/CCPA and State Laws)

Residents of certain U.S. states (including California, Colorado, Virginia, Connecticut, Utah) have rights regarding personal data. You have the right to:
  • Know what data is collected
  • Request deletion
  • Opt-out of sale or sharing of personal data
  • Limit use of sensitive data
We do NOT sell personal information in the traditional sense. However, ad networks may use data for cross-context behavioral advertising. You may opt-out via:
  • In-app privacy settings
  • Resetting your Advertising ID on your device
  • Device “Opt out of Ads Personalization” setting
This acts as a Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information request.
